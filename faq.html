Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our virtual try-on experience

General Questions

What is ASAP Virtual Try-On?

ASAP Virtual Try-On is an innovative platform that allows you to try on clothing and accessories virtually using augmented reality technology. Our platform uses Snapchat's Lens Studio to provide a seamless and realistic try-on experience right from your smartphone.

How does virtual try-on work?

Our virtual try-on technology uses advanced AR to overlay clothing items onto your camera feed in real-time. The system analyzes your body position and movements to ensure the clothing fits naturally. You can move around, turn, and see how the items look from different angles.

What devices are supported?

Our virtual try-on experience is optimized for mobile devices, particularly smartphones with AR capabilities. We recommend using the latest versions of iOS or Android devices for the best experience. The platform works through your device's camera and requires Snapchat to be installed.

Technical Questions

Do I need to install any special apps?

Yes, you need to have Snapchat installed on your device to use our virtual try-on feature. The AR experiences are powered by Snapchat's Lens Studio technology, which provides the most accurate and reliable try-on experience.

What are the camera requirements?

For the best experience, we recommend using a device with a high-quality camera and good lighting conditions. The camera should be able to clearly capture your full body for accurate try-on results. Make sure you're in a well-lit area with enough space to move around.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Our platform doesn't store any personal data or images. The AR try-on experience happens entirely on your device in real-time. We use secure connections and follow best practices for data protection.

Usage Questions

How do I try on items?

To try on items, simply browse our product catalog, select an item you're interested in, and click the "Try On" button. This will open the AR experience in Snapchat. Follow the on-screen instructions to position yourself and see how the item looks on you.

Can I save or share my try-on experience?

Yes! You can take photos and videos during your try-on experience and share them directly through Snapchat. This makes it easy to get feedback from friends or family before making a purchase decision.

What if the AR experience isn't working?

If you're experiencing issues, try these troubleshooting steps: 1) Ensure you have the latest version of Snapchat installed, 2) Check your camera permissions, 3) Make sure you have good lighting and enough space, 4) Try closing and reopening the app. If problems persist, contact our support team.
